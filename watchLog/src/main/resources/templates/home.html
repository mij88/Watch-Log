WELCOME TO WATCHLOG

Log and track all the shows you're watching or plan to watch!

You can track your shows by adding them to the following lists:

  • Show List: Shows you're planning to watch in the future.
  • Watching Now: Shows you're currently watching.
  • Paused Shows: Shows you've paused and plan to continue later.
  • Dropped Shows: Shows you have dropped and don't plan to watch.

You can move shows between lists by clicking on the appropriate buttons: Watching , Pause , Drop .
